About Me

Hello, my name is Thomas Fredricks. I attend Riverdale High School, and I plan to study Data Science at Drexel University in the fall. I enjoy reading, gaming, swimming, the outdoors, and messing around with programming and modeling. I founded and run the D&D club at Riverdale, and run games for my friends. I am a member of Post 58, and am one of the Service Coordinators for the Steering Committee. Through Post 58, I climbed Mt. St. Helens and Mt. Hood, sailed in the San Juan Islands, and trekked for two weeks in the Himalayas (no, I didn’t climb Everest, I don’t have a death wish). I have swam on the varsity team for Riverdale for four years and have been the acting team captain for three years. I am the Rules/Strategy/Marketing captain for our high school robotics team, and I attended competitions all four years.
